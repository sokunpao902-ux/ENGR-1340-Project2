About

What it offers

    1. Micro-Tasks


    A way for students to get real experience and get paid a fee. For companies, it allows them to get simple tasks done and make more people familiar with work in the company.

    2. Find People


    A way for students to interact with fellow students as well as companies. Companies get to reach out to any student they think would fit their company.

    3. Courses


    Video tutorials as well as live tutoring to help students learn skills they need. There are both free and premium courses to choose from.

    4. Success Tools


    Tools that allows companies to filter through data to find the best possible candidate. For students, it allows them to filter through data to find companies that are likely to hire them.
